Spongebob WhatsApp Status bot for breaking the fast (Iftar) using n8n and WAHA.

Built with n8n and WAHA:

1.Images are uploaded to Google Drive.
2. n8n retrieves the image data from Google Drive.
3. At the time of the Maghrib call to prayer (Adhan), n8n automatically posts a Spongebob status image corresponding to the specific date of Ramadan and the Maghrib schedule.
